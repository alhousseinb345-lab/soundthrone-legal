There are two ways to enter, each treated equally:

3416 Ebb Cir

After the Contest Period closes, the top ten (10) finalists from each state (by composite score from Sound Throne's eight-judge AI panel) are evaluated by an automated meta-judge ("THE THRONE") which selects one (1) Grand Prize Winner per state. AI verdicts are final.

Prizes paid by ACH bank transfer or check within 30 days after winner verification.

ARV: up to $10,000 per state. Total ARV per week: up to $470,000 (47 states × $10,000), assuming maximum participation. Most weeks the actual prize per state will be less. Odds of winning depend on the number of entries received per state.
